Lambda Beta Chapter - Alumni

Chapter Name: The Brazen Legion of Success

Welcome to the active alumni body for the North Florida region of Lambda Theta Phi Latin Fraternity, Inc. Here, alumni take it upon themselves to provide education, professional development opportunities, and a place for undergraduate, graduate, and professionals to continue to foster growth.

In addition to these responsibilities the board also works closely with Florida Sector One to coordinate statewide events such as annual banquets, conference and socials. Taking advantage of our huge alumni presence in northern Florida, Brothers are able to stay involved, network and meet brothers that reside in our vicinity. Involvement can vary from position in the chapter, position on sector, mentoring an undergraduate chapter, or just supporting local chapter and sector events. As alumni we have lots of experience and knowledge to give back to our undergraduates and our community.

Filling The Void When There Was A Need, Creating A Foundation For Others To Succeed.
