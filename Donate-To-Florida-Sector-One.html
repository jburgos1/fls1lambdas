What is Lambda Theta Phi Latin Fraternity, Inc.?

Lambda Theta Phi Latin Fraternity, Inc. is a non-profit social fraternity open to all college men who support our values of scholarship, respect for all cultures, community service, and the advancement and fair treatment of Latinos in the United States.
