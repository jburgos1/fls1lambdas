Let's talk!

If you have any questions regarding Lambda Theta Phi Latin Fraternity, Inc. at your school, want to bring Lambda to your campus, or just want to say hello; don't hesitate to contact us! We'd love to hear from you!

We exist to promote the spirit of brotherhood, to protect the rights of Latino students on their quest for education, to promote culture, and to maximize leadership in our communities. Send us a few lines, and we will gladly provide with your requested information.
