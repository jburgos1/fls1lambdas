About us

Florida Sector 1

The creation of a sector in the state of Florida gave more structure for our chapters and help with our expanding efforts at the time. The sector functioned as a state wide entity from 1995 until 2000. On a national level, we currently have over 20 sectors nationwide providing assitance and guidance to all our undergraduate and graduate chapters.

The Florida Sector 1 helps develop the leadership from chapters as well as their professionalism through different involvements such as the Lambda Leadership Series and the Future Leaders Retreat. The sector serves as a voice to the National Executive Board and carries on any communication between the both. As the first sector in the south, this sector also creates new programming and raises standards for the chapters to follow and maintain in their own campuses, and will continue to do in the future to keep the brotherhood.

Originally, there was only one sector in the state, but our leadership led the expansion within our state and we grew in size. For that reason, the state was split into two sectors, Florida Sector 1 and Florida Sector 2. .
