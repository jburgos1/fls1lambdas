Our Executive Board

In Unity There is Strength! is our motto and one that all current and past Executive Boards understood and practiced. We are a Sector moving in one direction and that is to develop and nurture future leaders for our nation!

As leaders of the Latino Greek Movement, we practice our ideals of Unity, Respect, Brotherhoood, Culture and Pride with unwavering commitment through our chapters for the benefit of our communities.

In the years 2013-2015, this team of brothers took on this responsiblity and will continue to instill our ideals to the younger leaders. Get to know them:

Jose Mugaburu

Sector Vice President

"Crossing is not an accomplishment but a life long commitment"

Jose comes from a Spanish-Peruvian background. He obtained his B.A. in Industrial and Systems Engineering from the University of Florida and his M.A. in Systems Engineering at Pennsylvania State University. He joined in the Spring of 2009 and since then has remain active involved in leadership position within the fraternity.
