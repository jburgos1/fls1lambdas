Jose Mugaburu

Sector Vice President

Jose Mugaburu was born in Panama, but from a Spanish-Peruvian background. He graduated from the University of Florida with a Bachelor in Industrial and Systems Engineering, and is currently pursuing a Master degree in Systems Engineering at Pennsylvania State University. Having only lived in the US for less than 7 years, he wanted a group of friends that provide the reliability that only a family could provide. For that and many other reasons, he joined the fraternity during the Spring 2009 semester, and has since been involved with the Phi chapter. He helped his chapter in many different ways surpassing obstacles and enjoying the good times. He renewed relationships and set high standards for his own chapter. In 2011, he jumped to a bigger role becoming part of the Sector Executive Board. And, will continue to be involved because he believes the fraternity can help brothers self-develop by taking advantage of the leadership and networking opportunities to further enhance the growth of brotherhood.He also advocates its meaning and is characterized by it, through high and lows, when asked by others. Today, he wants to continue to lead others by example into growing as the future leaders of the fraternity that offered him a ‘Home away from Home.’

Chapter: Phi, University of Florida Pledge Semester: Spring 2009
