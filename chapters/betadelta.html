BETA DELTA CHAPTER :: EMBRY RIDDLE AERONAUTICAL UNIVERSITY

Chapter Name: The Celestial Gates of Olympia

Founding Date: Fall 1999

Beta Delta Chapter was originally founded in fall 1999 by four students at Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University. After being on campus for two years the chapter died out. It was inactive for eleven years. During that time there was no presence of a Latino Fraternity on campus until 2010. Four students of Embry-Riddle became interested gentlemen and learned what it took to become a Lambda interested gentlemen. The gentlemen afterward pledged Lambda Theta Phi Latin Fraternity Incorporated and were able to bring Latino programming to Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University once again. The chapter was re-founded by these four gentlemen. Through the years they have participated in numerous events on campus. Beta delta chapter participated in Embry Riddle’s first Multicultural Greek Showcase by performing alpha salute, national salute and their chapter salute. They participated in “The walk for children’s diabetes” and “Collegiate Kindness day”. Beta Delta contributed community services hours to help the organization Children’s Tree house. They provided landscaping around its grounds and helped the providers at the facility.

Lambda Theta Phi Beta Delta Chapter, participates every year in Hispanic heritage month programming and provide cultural workshops to the student body. They believe in bridging the gap between cultural organizations and lead in help bring campus organizations together. Their presence is known on campus for being selfless by helping any student in need. Beta Delta chapter provides nickel drive fundraisers for the American Heart Association. With the work of the chapter they were able to raise money to help cure heart disease each year. Beta Delta chapter is strong and is here to stay!
