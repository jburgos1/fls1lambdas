Florida Sector 1 History

Nationally, Lambda Theta Phi Latin Fraternity, Inc. has over 20 sectors providing assistance and guidance to all our undergraduate and graduate chapters. The creation of a sector in the state of Florida gave structure to our chapters and helped with our expanding efforts at the time. Established in 1995, there was one sector for the state of Florida. As our sector expanded throughout the state, our leadership decided to split the larger sector into two: creating Florida Sector 1 and Florida Sector 2.

Florida Sector 1 helps develop chapter leadership as well as professionalism through different programming such as the Lambda Leadership Series and the Future Leaders Retreat. The sector serves as a voice to the National Executive Board and carries on any communication between both as well as serves as a governing body to all chapters located in north, central and west Florida. As the first sector in the south, we create new initiatives and raise standards for our chapters to follow and maintain on their own campuses. We have also raised the standards for Sectors across the nation. Nationally, we've won Sector of the Year 3 times (2010, 2011, & 2014).

Currently, the sector membership consists of five (5) undergaduate chapters:

Phi
Alpha Alpha
Alpha Psi
Beta Delta
Beta Iota

Lambda Leadership Series

LLS, as we call it, is a series of weekends during the Fall and Spring semesters. Brothers get together to attend and discuss fraternal business, develop our leadership and learn from guest speakers.

Future Leaders Retreat

FLR serves as a weekend for interested gentlemen to experience first-hand what it means to be part of our fraternity. This weekend includes professional enhancement and social events.
